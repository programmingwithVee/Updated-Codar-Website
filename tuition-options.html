tuition options

Education within your reach

Codar helps provide a direct path to a high paying tech job—without the time commitments and financial risks of traditional higher education. Our tuition payment options enable you to fund enrollment in Codar’s quality programs in a way that makes sense for your circumstances, needs, and ambitions. It's time to work toward a career that is both fulfilling and financially rewarding.

Codar's Payment Tuition

You already have the motivation and determination to land a high paying tech job—and transform your financial future. Now it’s time to figure out how to pay for the programs that will get you there. You can pay the tuition in a single payment, or pay broken into 3 installments, as shown below.

pay tuition in one payment

Once you are enrolled and have selected the upfront tuition option, you will receive an emailed invoice that you can pay by ACH, wire, credit card, or check. To make an ACH payment use the link in your invoice email. To make a wire or check payment, follow the instructions in the invoice email.

pay tuition in 3 installments

With this option, tuition is divided into 3 equal installments . Your first payment will be due by the enrollment deadline. The timing of the following 2 payments amd the amount that you pay will vary depending on where you live and your Codar’s program
